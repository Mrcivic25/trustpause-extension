Welcome to TrustPause!

Thank you for installing TrustPause. Your browser is now protected against phishing, tech support scams, and malicious domains.

We analyze the websites you visit to ensure you stay safe, and we never track your personal information or sell your data.

🛡️ Link to the Family Dashboard

TrustPause works best when paired with the Family Dashboard. If a family member or caretaker set this up for you, you can link this extension to their dashboard. This allows them to receive alerts if you stumble onto a dangerous website, and they can help manage your safe website list remotely.

How to link: Click the TrustPause icon in your browser toolbar (top right), enter the pairing code provided by your family member, and click "Link to Dashboard".
